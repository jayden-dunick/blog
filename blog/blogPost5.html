Concept


What is the theme and content of your event and what is it's purpose?


My event is a point A to B off road race without a set route, contestants will attempt to find their own routes and aim for the best time to get a prize. Within the website they can view a leaderboard and a map plotting their routes.



Who is the audience for your event, and why and how will they attend?


My event is targeted towards male 26-35 year olds, who have an intrest in off road racing. They'll either be able to drive to the event or catch a shuttle going towards there.
